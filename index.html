About Me

I'm an Assistant Professor at Bryn Mawr College. I graduated with a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Pennsylvania in December 2023 advised by Professor Mayur Naik. I received my B.S. in computer science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2018. I work on synergies between program reasoning tools and neural models for software engineering tasks.[Research Statement]
